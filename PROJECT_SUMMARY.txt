--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
USAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Build:
   docker build -t bookmark-generator:latest .

2. Deploy:
   helm install bookmark-generator ./helm/bookmark-generator

3. Access:
   kubectl port-forward svc/bookmark-generator 8080:80
   Visit http://localhost:8080

4. Download:
   - Click "Download for Firefox" or "Download for Chrome"
   - Import into your browser
